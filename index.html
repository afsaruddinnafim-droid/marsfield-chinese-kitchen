Our Story

A kitchen rooted in family, community & real flavour

Marsfield Chinese Kitchen has been feeding the Ryde community for decades. What started as a small family venture on Trafalgar Place has grown into one of the neighbourhood's most beloved dining institutions — a place where the same regulars come back week after week, and where new faces quickly become familiar ones.

Our recipes haven't changed much, and that's exactly the point. We cook the way our family has always cooked: generously portioned, honestly priced, and made fresh to order. Whether you're grabbing a quick lunch special, ordering a feast for the whole family, or picking up your usual on a Friday night, you'll always be treated like a regular here.

We're proud to be a local institution in Marsfield — and even prouder that so many of you have made us part of your family's routine.
